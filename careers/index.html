What makes life at Air Apps different?

Global Multi-Cultural Team

Talent has no nationality. We connect awesome and gifted team players across the globe to deliver exceptional work and an extraordinary customer experience.

Work Remotely

A fully-remote company means flexible schedules thatll help you manage your personal and professional life.

Life & Performance Coach

We proactively support our team members in improving their day-to-day lives, optimizing their minds and bodies to live well, achieving their goals and performing at their very best.

Transparent Environment

At Air Apps, "sharing is caring". We foster open communication to share knowledge and help each other.

Flexibility

Work anywhere, anytime. At Air Apps, you have the freedom to choose where and when to work.

Collaboration

We support each other and are willing to learn from others. We thrive on feedback and every point of view is respected. We share our goals and contribute together to achieve them, as a team should.

Community

Were committed to creating a community where everyone feels heard, appreciated and safe to grow as a professional while being part of a thriving company.

Air Get-together

Once a year, our team meets up in a beautiful location across the world for a week of work, team bonding and development.

Work-Life Fluidity

We want our team to work in the way they know works best for them. When it comes to working and personal life, it is more important to be flexible and fluid than to try to place everyone into a strict, time-limited box.

Benefits

Our crew are at the center of everything we do.
We want them to be at their best. Thats why we offer:

Health Insurance

We take care of our team, offering health insurance to cover medical expenses in the US and other countries.

Paid time off

Team members get a minimum of 24 non-consecutive days - we adapt depending on the countrys standards. We want team members to recharge their batteries and show up to do their best work.

Sick Leave

If one of our team members or a member of their family requiring their care falls ill, we offer sick leave. Were a community that supports our team members and cares about their health and wellness.

Parental Leave

4 weeks of 100% paid time off during the first year of parenthood or more if otherwise required by local law.

Pregnancy Leave

8 weeks of 100% paid time off during pregnancy, or within one year from giving birth or more if otherwise required by local law.

Bereavement Leave

In the event of a death, team members are entitled to seven consecutive days of paid leave or more if otherwise required by local law.

National Holidays

We value our international team and provide any local National Holidays as paid time off, or more if otherwise required by local law.

Enrichment Stipend

Personal growth and improvement are part of our culture. We have a monthly stipend focused on wellness, personal growth and improvement expenses.

Learning & Development

Every team member has a yearly stipend to partake in learning and development programs.

Home Office Setup

Having the right equipment is crucial to being effective at work. Thats why we offer a home office stipend - available within the first 45 days of your employment and every two years after that.

Connect with a Team Member

We encourage collaboration and socialization. We provide everyone with a budget per quarter that can be used to visit a team member or organize a small get-together.
